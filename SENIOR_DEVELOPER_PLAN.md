## ğŸ“ Certification Exam Tips

### **General Strategy:**
1. **Read official exam objectives** - Know what's tested
2. **Hands-on practice is crucial** - Don't just watch videos
3. **Take multiple practice exams** - Get comfortable with format
4. **Schedule exam in advance** - Creates accountability
5. **Morning exams are best** - When you're fresh

### **Microsoft Azure Exams:**
- Questions are scenario-based
- Often multiple "correct" answers (choose BEST)
- Case studies at the beginning (skip and return)
- Flag uncertain questions for review
- Time management: ~2 minutes per question

### **TOGAF Exams:**
- Foundation: 40 questions, 60 min, 55% to pass
- Certified: 8 complex scenarios, 90 min, 60% to pass
- Read questions carefully (tricky wording)
- Use official terminology
- Understand WHY, not just WHAT
